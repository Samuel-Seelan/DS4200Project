SFO Air Traffic Passenger Flow (1999–2023)

  • Home

This website explores trends in air travel through San Francisco International Airport (SFO) from 1999 to 2023. We use public datasets and interactive visualizations to uncover how airline performance, geographic patterns, and major events like COVID-19 affected passenger flow.

Our analysis combines traditional plots and interactive tools built with Altair, Plotly, and D3.js. You'll also find a comparison between COVID-19 hospitalization trends and air travel.
